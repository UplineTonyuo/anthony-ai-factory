Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

This Privacy Policy explains how Anthony AI Factory ("we", "us", the "Service") handles information in connection with the Service. Anthony AI Factory is currently in development. This policy will be updated as the Service evolves.

Information you provide directly

We may collect information that you choose to provide directly, such as your name, email address, or other contact details, for example when you sign up for updates, create an account, or contact us.

Information from connected third-party platforms

If you choose to connect a third-party social media account (such as TikTok or other supported platforms) to the Service, we may receive certain information from that platform, but only after you have explicitly authorized the connection and only to the extent of the permissions ("scopes") you grant during that authorization process. What is shared depends entirely on the platform and the permissions you approve — we do not receive more than what the platform's authorization flow allows.

Account and profile information

Where relevant to the features you use, this may include basic profile information made available by a connected platform, such as a display name, username, or profile identifier, so that the Service can identify which connected account an action applies to.

Content and media information

The Service is designed to help manage content you create or provide, which may include text, images, video, captions, and related media files, so that this content can be organized, reviewed, and published through the workflows you configure.

Publishing-related information

Where you use the Service to schedule or publish content to a connected platform, we may process information related to that publishing action, such as the status of a post, timestamps, and identifiers returned by the platform, in order to show you the outcome of the action you requested.

Analytics and performance information

Where a connected platform makes performance or engagement metrics available for your own content (for example, views, likes, or comments), and you have authorized access to that data, the Service may retrieve and display this information back to you for your own review.

OAuth authorization

Connections to third-party platforms are established using each platform's own authorization process (commonly known as OAuth). You control which permissions are granted at the time of authorization, and you can review or revoke those permissions through the third-party platform's own account settings at any time.

How information is used

We use the information described above to:

  • operate and provide the features of the Service that you use;
  • enable content creation, review, and approval workflows;
  • carry out publishing actions that you specifically request;
  • display performance or analytics information that you have authorized access to;
  • maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Service;
  • communicate with you about the Service, if you have provided contact information.

Service providers and third-party platforms

We may rely on third-party service providers to help operate parts of the Service (for example, hosting infrastructure). Where the Service integrates with third-party social media platforms, those platforms operate under their own privacy policies and terms, which apply to their handling of your data. Use of the Service does not imply any endorsement, partnership, or affiliation with any third-party platform, including TikTok, Meta, Instagram, or Facebook.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service to you, to comply with applicable legal obligations, and to resolve disputes. Because the Service and its underlying architecture are still being developed, specific retention periods and storage mechanisms are not yet finalized and may change as the Service matures.

Data deletion requests

You may request deletion of your information by contacting us using the details below. We will make reasonable efforts to respond to verified deletion requests, subject to any legal or technical limitations.

Disconnecting a connected account

You may disconnect a connected third-party account from the Service at any time, and you may also revoke the Service's access directly through the third-party platform's own account or app-permission settings. Disconnection stops future access under that authorization; it does not necessarily delete information already processed by the Service, unless you separately request deletion.

Security

We aim to use reasonable measures to help protect information processed by the Service. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so that we can address it.

International users

The Service may be accessed from various countries. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed in a country other than your own, which may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service develops. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
